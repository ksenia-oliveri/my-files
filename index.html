Nikola Tesla

The man who change the world

Here's a time line of Tesla's life:

  • 1859

    Born to a Serbian Family in Smiljan

  • 1870

    Tesla contracted cholera. He was in hospital for close to one year, and he faced near-death circumstances.

  • 1875

    Tesla enrolls in college

  • 1880

    Tesla moves to Prague to study Philosophy. He took up classes at the university, though he did not receive grades for the courses.

  • 1881

    He moved to Budapest in Hungary, to work for a telegraph company.

  • 1882

    Tesla goes to work for Edison. He began working in a new industry by installing indoor lighting resources.

  • 1885

    Tesla submits some of his first patents.

  • 1887

    Tesla re-imagines the electric motor.

  • 1890

    Tesla begins his work on wireless power transmission. He researched various approaches to transmitting power by inductive and capacitive coupling.

  • 1891

    Tesla patents the Tesla Coil

  • 1943

    at age 86, Tesla died in a Hotel Room in New York.
